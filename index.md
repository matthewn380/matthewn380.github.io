# 🎧 PodSync - Sync your music to classic iPods

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-PodSync-blue.svg)](https://github.com/matthewn380/PodSync)

PodSync lets you move your music from your computer to your classic iPod. This tool works with your digital music files and transfers them to your device. You keep control of your media collection without paid subscriptions or complicated software.

## 🛠 Features

PodSync simplifies how you interact with your classic hardware.

*   **Drag-and-Drop Syncing:** You move music by dragging folders directly into the app window. The app handles the transfer.
*   **Media Support:** The app manages music, podcasts, and audiobooks. It reads the files on your computer and writes them to the iPod format.
*   **Last.fm Support:** You can track what you listen to. The app connects to your Last.fm account to log your plays.
*   **Open Source:** You see the code behind the app. This ensures transparency and removes hidden costs.

## ⚙️ How to use the app

Connect your iPod to your computer using a USB cable before you open PodSync. The application detects the device as soon as you plug it in.

Once the app shows your device, follow these steps:

1. Find the music folder on your computer.
2. Click and hold the folder.
3. Drag the folder into the main area of the PodSync window.
4. Wait for the progress bar to finish.
5. Safely eject the iPod from your computer after the transfer completes.

The app formats your music files into the standard format that iPods expect. You do not need to convert files manually. The app handles this process in the background.

## 💻 System requirements

PodSync runs on modern computer systems. Ensure you meet these requirements for the best performance:

*   **Operating System:** Windows 10 or Windows 11.
*   **Memory:** At least 4GB of RAM is recommended.
*   **Processor:** Any modern dual-core processor.
*   **Connection:** A working USB port for your iPod.

Check that your computer installs all pending system updates before you start. This prevents issues with driver recognition for your device.

## 📁 Troubleshooting and tips

If your computer fails to see the iPod, try these steps:

*   **Check the cable:** Use a different USB cable if the computer does not notice the connection.
*   **Restart the device:** Force restart the iPod to clear memory issues.
*   **Check port connection:** Plug the cable directly into the computer rather than a USB hub.
*   **Update drivers:** Open your device manager to ensure the computer recognizes the iPod storage.

Contact the repository maintainer through the project page if you encounter bugs or errors. Include the error message and the version number of your app to speed up the support process.
